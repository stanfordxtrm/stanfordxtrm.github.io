About

XTRM (pronounced "extreme") is Stanford University's Korean and East-Asian Pop Fusion Dance Team, which debuted in 2013 at the Korean Student Association's culture show. Since then, XTRM has performed for various Stanford functions, held its own showcase, and recently garnered 2nd place at the 2015 San Francisco K-pop World Festival. Not only does XTRM strive to represent elements of East-Asian pop culture, but also to provide the community with the opportunity to learn new dance styles that have been popularized by various K-pop groups, such as SNSD, 2NE1, 4Minute, GOT7, BTS, and EXO. We welcome members from all backgrounds who are interested in dance and East-Asian pop culture.

Join Us

Want to dance with us? Join our listserve to receive the most up-to-date announcements about our practices. Many of our members had zero to minimal dance experience before joining us, so dancers of all levels (including beginners) are welcome to come to our workshops!

You can also join our group on Stanford's OrgSync page!
